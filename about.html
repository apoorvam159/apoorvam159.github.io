Hello! I'm Apoorva, and I am set to graduate from
the University of Colorado Boulder in May 2025 with a
Bachelor's degree in Environmental Product Design from the
ENVD school. My academic journey has equipped me
with a major in Product Design and a minor in
Creative Technology and Design. Additionally, I am in
the process of earning a Google Certificate in UX Design.

Over the past four years, I have honed my skills in various
tools within the Adobe Creative Cloud, including
Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop. I utilize Figma
for my app designs and have experimented with Adobe XD as well.
My experience spans user and market research, as well as
prototyping, both through my college projects and personal endeavors.

I am deeply passionate about app and web design and am
eager to advance my career in this field. I love learning
new skills and rapidly adapt to different hobbies. While I enjoy
exploring new activities, my favorite pastimes are often home-based.
I love to crochet, and paint, and have recently taken up cross-stitching.
